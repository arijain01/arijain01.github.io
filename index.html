Arihant Jain

I am a PhD student at McGill University working in Number Theory under the supervision of Prof Henri Darmon .

I was previously a Master's student at Concordia University. I worked under the supervision of Prof Chantal David, where I worked on computing Moments of L-functions attached to Cubic characters. Before that, I completed my Bachelor's in Mathematics from IIT Kanpur where I learned a lot of Mathematics from Prof Amit Kuber, Prof Somnath Jha and Mr. Himanshu Shukla.
